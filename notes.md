Website Name - CalcuFarm | Your Perfect Hydroponics Setup Buddy

Target Audience - People planning to do hydroponics or farming in their homes who need help getting wut they need and how they can do it. They usually dont know where to start or what to buy so this website will help them with that.

Purpose - 
To make the process of setup cost estimation easier and more accurate. Users can input their requirements and get a detailed breakdown of the costs involved in setting up their hydroponics or farming system. This eliminates the need for manual calculations and provides a clear picture of the investment required.

Design Language - minimalist, elegant , green , leaf theme , looking like a plant

Sections - 
Hero - People dont know what to do and how to start hydroponics so this website provides a guide at starting with a index of the needed and optional items , cart option where there will be a - calculator to add and give the total price.
Testimonials - autoscrolling reviews of people who used it 
Footer - Calculate with 10,000 other people!!
Color - every shade of green avialable - 40% , White - 50% , Blue - 10%
Mood - calm , peaceful , trustworthy , elegant ,tranquil , easy to use , premium ,  up-to-date prices , all optional items available


The Basic Procedure - The user selects a system size (Small / Medium / Large) and ticks optional add-ons (grow lights, water pump, pH test kit, starter nutrients) using checkboxes. As they make choices, show a running total of the setup cost and an estimated monthly running cost, plus a clear itemised price breakdown. Use preset prices defined in JavaScript. Keep the design clean and trustworthy with green accents and clear typography.
